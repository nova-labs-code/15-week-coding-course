Week 17: Content & SEO Basics

This week focuses on **optimizing content and search engine strategies** to make your site discoverable and engaging.

Day 1: Keyword Research

Learn: Identify keywords your audience searches for using free tools or brainstorming. Focus on relevance and search volume. Do: Make a list of 10–15 keywords related to your site topic. Check: Keywords should align with your site purpose and audience.

Day 2: On-Page SEO

Learn: Optimize page elements like <title>, headings (<h1>, <h2>), alt text for images, and URLs. Do: Take one page and rewrite the title, headings, and image alt text using your target keywords. Check: Keywords appear naturally without stuffing.

Day 3: Internal Linking

Learn: Connect related pages to guide users and search engines. Internal links improve navigation and SEO. Do: Add links between your pages (e.g., Home → About → Contact). Check: Links should be logical, not broken, and clearly labeled.

Day 4: Content Formatting

Learn: Use headings, lists, bold/italics, images, and multimedia to make content readable. Do: Reformat a page or article using headings and lists for clarity. Check: Anyone scanning the page should understand the main points quickly.

Day 5: Performance Optimization

Learn: Fast pages improve SEO and user experience. Compress images, minimize code, and reduce unnecessary scripts. Do: Compress images on one page and remove unused code. Check: Page loads noticeably faster and still displays correctly.

Day 6: Analytics Setup

Learn: Track user behavior with tools like Google Analytics. Add tracking code to pages. Do: Set up analytics and check real-time traffic when visiting your site. Check: You should see pageviews and user actions recorded in the dashboard.

Day 7: Review & Adjust

Learn: Check SEO elements, content formatting, keywords, and site speed. Do: Make a checklist and adjust weak points. Check: You should have at least 3 pages optimized and ready for promotion.
