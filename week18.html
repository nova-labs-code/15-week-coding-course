Week 18: Promotion & Social Growth

This week focuses on **sharing your site, growing an audience, and measuring engagement** effectively.

Day 1: Social Media Basics

Learn: Choose platforms where your audience is active. Each platform has different strengths. Do: Create accounts for your site on 1–2 social media platforms. Check: Accounts should have profile info, link to your site, and branding consistent with your website.

Day 2: Shareable Content

Learn: Content that is engaging, visual, or interactive gets shared more. Do: Create one social post promoting your site with image/video and a clear call to action. Check: Post looks appealing and communicates the message quickly.

Day 3: Email Marketing

Learn: Collect subscribers with opt-in forms and send newsletters. Do: Add a simple signup form on your site and plan the first email with welcome message. Check: Submissions work and you have a draft email ready.

Day 4: Community Engagement

Learn: Encourage interaction through comments, polls, or Q&A. Engagement keeps visitors returning. Do: Add a comment form or small poll to one page. Check: Test form/poll submissions and review responses.

Day 5: Collaborations & Guest Content

Learn: Partner with others or invite guest posts to expand audience reach. Do: Identify 1–2 potential collaborators or guest content ideas. Check: Make a contact plan or outline collaboration proposal.

Day 6: Track Promotion

Learn: Measure clicks, shares, and traffic sources to see what works. Do: Use analytics to check which social posts or links drive traffic. Check: Identify at least one post performing well and one that needs improvement.

Day 7: Weekly Review

Learn: Evaluate promotion effectiveness and plan improvements. Do: Adjust strategy based on analytics and feedback, update social content calendar. Check: You should have a clear plan for growing your site audience over the next weeks.
