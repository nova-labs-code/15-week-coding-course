Week 18: Promotion & Social Growth – Extended

This week focuses on **sharing your site, building an audience, and measuring engagement effectively**. Each day combines practical steps with exercises and tips to grow your online presence.

Day 1: Social Media Basics

Learn: Pick platforms your audience uses most. Each platform (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Twitter/X, Facebook) has different strengths. Consistency and branding matter.

Do: Create accounts for your site on 1–2 social platforms. Add profile info, link to your site, and maintain consistent branding (logo, colors, style).

Mini Exercise: Design a profile picture, header, and bio that match your website theme. Post one introductory post.
Tip: Use the same handle if possible across platforms for recognition.
Day 2: Shareable Content

Learn: Content that is visual, interactive, or informative gets shared more. Add clear calls-to-action like “Visit our site” or “Comment below”.

Do: Create one post promoting your site with an image or short video. Make it visually appealing and include text highlighting benefits or value.

Mini Exercise: Draft 3 alternative captions for the post and test which looks most engaging to friends or peers.
Tip: Keep text concise and attention-grabbing; most users scroll quickly.
Day 3: Email Marketing

Learn: Collect subscribers via opt-in forms. Send newsletters, updates, or promotions. Personalized emails often perform best.

Do: Add a simple email signup form on your website. Draft a welcome email for new subscribers including a link to your best content.

Mini Exercise: Test submitting an email; ensure the signup works and the welcome email is readable and professional.
Tip: Avoid sending too frequently; 1–2 emails per week is optimal for engagement.
Day 4: Community Engagement

Learn: Interactions like comments, polls, or Q&A encourage repeat visits. Responding to your audience builds loyalty.

Do: Add a comment section or small poll on one page. Ask 1–2 questions related to your content.

Mini Exercise: Invite 2–3 friends to submit responses and reply to their answers. Note engagement trends.
Tip: Keep questions short and easy to answer; use multiple-choice for simplicity if needed.
Day 5: Collaborations & Guest Content

Learn: Partnering with others or accepting guest posts can increase reach. Identify influencers, bloggers, or related sites in your niche.

Do: List 1–3 potential collaborators. Outline a guest post idea or co-promotional plan.

Mini Exercise: Draft a polite outreach email proposing collaboration or guest content.
Tip: Focus on mutual benefit; show how the collaboration benefits both parties.
Day 6: Tracking Promotion

Learn: Analytics show which posts or campaigns drive traffic and conversions. Tools: Google Analytics, social platform insights, or UTM links.

Do: Track clicks, pageviews, and user interactions from your promotion efforts. Identify which channels perform best.

Mini Exercise: Review last week’s social posts and website visits; note which content led to the most engagement.
Tip: Focus future promotion on high-performing content for better ROI.
Day 7: Weekly Review & Strategy

Learn: Combine all promotion efforts into a cohesive strategy. Adjust posting schedule, content type, and channels based on performance data.

Do: Update your content calendar for the next month including social posts, email campaigns, and collaborations. Prioritize high-performing channels.

Mini Exercise: Prepare a report summarizing analytics, engagement trends, and action points for next week.
Tip: Review regularly; promotion strategy should evolve based on audience behavior and feedback.
